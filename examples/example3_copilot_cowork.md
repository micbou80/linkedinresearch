📢 Introducing Copilot Cowork - A new way of getting work done ✨

Microsoft 365 Copilot has been great at helping us find answers and draft content. With Copilot Cowork, it helps Copilot take action.⚡

🧠 With Copilot Cowork, you describe the outcome you want, and Cowork grounds the work in your emails, meetings, messages, files, and data, powered by Work IQ.

🧭 Cowork turns your request into a plan with clear checkpoints, so you can confirm progress, make changes, or pause execution at any time. Cowork checks in when it needs clarification.

Here are four examples of Cowork turning intent into actions 👇
1️⃣ Clean up your calendar: Reschedule meetings and protect focus time
2️⃣ Build the meeting packet and align the team: Generate the deck, document, and follow-up (This example 🎥)
3️⃣ Research a company fast: Pull sources, compile analysis, and package results
4️⃣ Create the launch plan: Build competitive intel and shareable assets

🧪 I’ve been testing this for the last couple of weeks and have been incredibly impressed with the quality of the responses and its ability to handle multiple complex tasks.

🔒 Copilot Cowork runs within Microsoft 365’s security and governance boundaries. Identity, permissions, and compliance policies apply by default, and actions and outputs are auditable.
