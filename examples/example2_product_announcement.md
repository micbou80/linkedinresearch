📢 Microsoft Frontier Transformation Announcement 7️⃣ Copilot in Word

✨ Copilot is now agentic in Word, collaborating with you to turn working drafts into review‑ready documents in the same file your team is already editing.

📝 Ask Copilot in Word to update or fill in a template-based doc, add an executive summary, and suggest edits that incorporate stakeholder feedback into a draft that’s ready to share.

🔁 Copilot in Word can restructure sections, rewrite for clarity, and apply Word-native styles so edits are easy to review. 

🧠 Work IQ grounding: Copilot can keep the document aligned to your latest work context (decisions, dates, and details), so you spend less time reconciling updates.

🎛️ Model choice: Model choice with OpenAI and Anthropic models for this editing experience will be available in April.

Examples (prompts in the comments 👇)
✅ Create a project status + decision brief
✅ Refresh a recurring monthly exec report
✅ Formatting and polish pass

🛣️ Generally available today in Word on Windows, web, and Mac for Microsoft 365 Copilot users.
