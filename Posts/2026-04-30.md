# The hidden cost of unmanaged Microsoft Teams Rooms — and the 10-minute audit that fixes it

## Hook
One unmanaged Teams Room costs you ~$1,200/month.

## Full Post
One unmanaged Teams Room costs you ~$1,200/month.

Not in licensing.
Not in hardware.
In wasted meeting time.

3–5 degraded or failed meetings per room, per month.
At $450/hour fully loaded, the math is brutal.

Most IT managers don't have a hardware problem.
They have a visibility problem.

They don't know which rooms are failing until someone complains.
By then, the damage — and the reputation hit on IT — is already done.

Here's what I see missing most often:

 - No CQD filtering by room UPN (so bad rooms hide in the averages)
 - Audio devices left on "Auto" (most common cause of inconsistent quality)
 - No utilisation data pulled in 60+ days (rooms no one uses still need managing)
 - Resource mailboxes misconfigured (rooms that don't show up in Outlook room finder)

None of these are hard to fix.
All of them are invisible until you look.

I put together a 10-minute Teams Rooms Health Audit checklist — 25 checks across device health, call quality, scheduling, and security.

It's the first thing I'd run if I inherited someone else's Teams Rooms environment.
