Most IT teams don't have a Teams Rooms problem.
They have a visibility problem.

The room looks fine until it isn't.
No alert fires. No ticket opens.
The CFO just tells everyone "the video system is broken again."

Here's what actually causes most failures:

 - Resource account password expired (silent, instant, invisible)
 - Device 3+ app versions behind — still shows Online in TAC
 - MFA Conditional Access applied to a room account (breaks sign-in overnight)
 - QoS not configured — call quality degrades under load, no one notices until a board call

None of these are hard to fix.
