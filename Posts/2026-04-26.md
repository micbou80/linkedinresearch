Your meeting rooms are costing you money every hour they sit empty.

The average mid-market org runs Teams Rooms at 38% utilisation.

That means 62% of the time, you're paying for hardware, real estate, and licences — for a room nobody booked.

At $420/month per room across 12 rooms, that's over $37,000 a year. Idle.

And that's before you count the friction tax.

23% of meeting room sessions have a tech issue at start.
6.3 minutes lost. 5 people waiting. Every time.

Most IT leaders don't have a Teams Rooms problem.
They have a measurement problem.

When you can't show the CFO a number, "upgrade the AV" stays at the bottom of the budget list.

When you can show them $49,000 in annual productivity loss — that's a different conversation.

 - Configure Room Finder in Outlook and Teams (most orgs haven't)
 - Set a monthly utilisation review using Teams Admin Center reports
