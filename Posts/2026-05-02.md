# The hidden cost of unmanaged Microsoft Teams Rooms — and how to fix it in one afternoon

## Hook
73% of meeting room faults are never reported to IT.

## Full Post
73% of meeting room faults are never reported to IT.

Users just work around them.
Bring a laptop. Skip the room system. Blame "bad tech."

And IT never finds out — until a VP complains in front of a client.

Most organisations don't have a Microsoft Teams Rooms problem.
They have a visibility problem.

The faults are there. Firmware that hasn't updated in 4 months. A room mic silently replaced by a laptop mic. A display that stopped syncing calendars three weeks ago.

None of it shows up in the helpdesk queue.
All of it shows up in the meeting experience.

The fix isn't complicated:
 - Enrol every room in Teams Rooms Pro Management (free monitoring tier exists)
 - Lock default audio config in XML so laptops can't override the room mic
 - Check licence assignments — user licences on room accounts break more than you'd expect
 - Schedule firmware pushes overnight, not reactively

I put together a 10-point Teams Rooms Health Audit checklist — takes about 15 minutes per room, and it catches the exact failures that erode hybrid meeting quality without ever triggering a ticket.
