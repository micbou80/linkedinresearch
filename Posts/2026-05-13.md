# The hidden cost of unmanaged Microsoft Teams Rooms — and how to fix it in one afternoon

## Hook
73% of meeting room tech failures are never reported to IT.

## Full Post
73% of meeting room tech failures are never reported to IT.

People don't complain. They just stop using the room.

They drag a laptop to the table. They crowd around one screen. They mute the room system and dial in on their phones.

You never see a ticket. You just see adoption numbers quietly flatline.

Most IT teams don't have a Microsoft Teams Rooms problem.
They have a silent failure problem.

The room looks fine on the dashboard. No alerts. No tickets.
But the camera hasn't auto-framed in three months.
The one-touch join stopped working after the last Exchange update.
And nobody told you — because they gave up.

I've put together a 10-point Teams Rooms Health Audit checklist.

Not theory. A room-by-room walkthrough:
 - License assigned correctly?
 - Calendar sync actually working?
 - Audio echo passing a live call test?
 - Firmware current?
 - Someone on-site who can restart it without calling IT?

Each check takes under 90 seconds. The full audit takes one afternoon.
