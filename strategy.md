## Active Hypothesis

## Voice & Tone

- Direct and practical — no fluff, no corporate-speak
- Personal experience over generic advice
- Honest about failures, not just wins
- Peer-to-peer: colleague sharing a discovery, not expert lecturing
- Specific > vague. Numbers > adjectives.

## Post Formula (Current Best Guess)

1. **Hook** (line 1): "I [did something specific] and [got concrete result]"
2. **Context** (2-3 lines): Why this matters to an IT pro right now
3. **Value Body**: 3-5 punchy, practical takeaways — white space between each line
4. **CTA**: Low friction — ask a question OR offer the lead magnet
5. **Hashtags**: 3-5 (#AI #Leadership #FutureOfWork #Productivity #TechCulture)

## Niche

IT professionals and enthusiasts who:
