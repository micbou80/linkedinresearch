### Why this exists

Most IT leaders know their meeting rooms are painful. Few can *prove* it in euros or dollars — which means budget requests get rejected.

This calculator gives you a defensible number to take to finance.

---

## Step 1: Count your broken minutes

**Fill in your numbers:**

| Variable | Your Number |
|---|---|
| Average meeting participants | _____ |
| Minutes lost per meeting (setup, reconnects, audio issues) | _____ |
| Meetings per room per day | _____ |
| Number of meeting rooms | _____ |
| Working days per year | 220 |

**Formula:**
```
Lost minutes/year = participants × lost_min × meetings/day × rooms × 220
```

**Example:**
- 6 participants × 8 min lost × 4 meetings × 10 rooms × 220 days
- = **422,400 minutes lost per year**
- = **7,040 hours**

---

## Step 2: Convert to salary cost

| Variable | Your Number |
|---|---|
| Average fully-loaded hourly cost per employee (€/hr) | _____ |

**Formula:**
```
Annual cost = (lost minutes ÷ 60) × hourly_rate
```

**Example (€65/hr blended rate):**
- 7,040 hours × €65 = **€457,600/year in wasted salary**
