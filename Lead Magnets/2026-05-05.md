## Step 2 — The Failure Rate Problem

Microsoft's own research shows **43% of hybrid meetings** experience a technical issue in the first 5 minutes.
Industry average: **7–10 minutes lost** per affected meeting to setup, cable-switching, or call quality issues.

| Calculation | Formula | Your Result |
|---|---|---|
| Meetings per room per year | Daily meetings × 220 working days | |
| Affected meetings (43%) | Total meetings × 0.43 | |
| Hours lost per room per year | Affected meetings × (8 min ÷ 60) | |
| Cost of lost time per room | Hours lost × avg attendees × hourly rate | |
| **Total cost across all rooms** | Cost per room × number of rooms | |

> **Example (10 rooms, 5 meetings/day, 6 attendees, €65/hr):**
> 10 rooms × 5 meetings × 220 days = 11,000 meetings/year
> 11,000 × 0.43 = 4,730 affected meetings
> 4,730 × (8÷60) hrs × 6 people × €65 = **€154,780 lost per year**
