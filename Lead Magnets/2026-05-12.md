# The Teams Rooms Health Audit Checklist: 15-Minute Room-by-Room Diagnostic for IT Managers

# The Teams Rooms Health Audit Checklist
## 15-Minute Room-by-Room Diagnostic for IT Managers

**Use this checklist once per quarter. One person. One afternoon. Catch issues before your CEO does.**

---

### Before You Start
- [ ] Export your Teams Rooms device inventory from Teams Admin Center (TAC)
- [ ] Note which rooms are flagged Unhealthy or Offline in TAC
- [ ] Identify your top 5 most-booked rooms (check room mailbox calendar or TAC usage reports)

> **Rule of thumb:** Audit your top 5 rooms first. They cause 80% of your support tickets.

---

### Section 1: Device & Hardware Health (per room)

- [ ] Device shows **Online** in Teams Admin Center
- [ ] Last check-in was **within 24 hours**
- [ ] App version is within **2 releases** of current (check: TAC > Devices > Teams Rooms)
- [ ] Windows OS updates are current (no pending restarts)
- [ ] Camera is detected and preview image is clean (no black screen, no IR glare)
- [ ] Microphone array passes loopback test — no echo, no hum
- [ ] Speaker output audible at 50% volume from back of room
- [ ] Display(s) power on automatically when meeting starts
- [ ] HDMI ingest / content cable present and working (test with a laptop)
- [ ] Room console (touchscreen) is responsive — no lag, no frozen screen

**Red flags that need same-day action:**
- Device offline > 48 hours
- App version 3+ releases behind (security risk)
- Camera black screen (driver or USB hub failure — common on Logitech Rally)

---

### Section 2: Network & Connectivity

- [ ] Room device is on a **dedicated VLAN** for AV/conferencing
- [ ] QoS policies are applied (DSCP marking for Teams media traffic)
- [ ] Confirm upload/download > 10 Mbps from room device (use speedtest via TAC remote or direct)
- [ ] No proxy or SSL inspection intercepting Teams traffic (check bypass rules)
- [ ] Wi-Fi rooms: signal strength > -65 dBm, 5GHz band confirmed
- [ ] Ethernet rooms: confirm Gigabit port, no duplex mismatch in switch logs

**Fill in the blank:**
Room __________ has been reporting call quality issues. Likely cause based on checklist: __________. Fix owner: __________. Target resolution date: __________.

---

### Section 3: Account & Licensing

- [ ] Room resource account has a **Teams Rooms Basic** or **Teams Rooms Pro** license assigned
- [ ] Resource account password is set to **never expire** (or managed by service account policy)
- [ ] Account is NOT MFA-enforced via Conditional Access (breaks sign-in — use exclusion group)
- [ ] Room mailbox **auto-accept** is enabled for meeting requests
- [ ] Room capacity is set correctly in Exchange (affects room finder)
- [ ] Account is not blocked or disabled in Entra ID

> **Common $0 fix:** 30% of Teams Rooms sign-in failures are caused by an expired resource account password. Check this first.

---

### Section 4: Meeting Experience Quality

- [ ] Join a test call from the room console — confirm audio/video in <10 seconds
- [ ] Front Row layout is enabled (if display supports it)
- [ ] Intelligent Speaker / voice recognition is configured (Pro license required)
- [ ] Content camera (whiteboard) is set up if whiteboard present in room
- [ ] Room shows correct time zone on console idle screen
- [ ] Scheduled meetings appear on console (confirms calendar sync is healthy)

---

### Section 5: Pro License ROI Check (skip if on Basic)

- [ ] Teams Rooms Pro Management Portal is active for this device
- [ ] At least 1 alert rule is configured (e.g., device offline > 1 hour → email to IT)
- [ ] Call Quality Dashboard (CQD) shows this room's calls in the last 30 days
- [ ] Meeting room analytics are reviewed monthly by someone on the IT team

**Is Pro worth it for this room?**
Use this threshold: If the room hosts >10 meetings/week or seats executives, Pro pays for itself in one prevented escalation. ($40/room/month vs. 2 hours of IT firefighting time.)

---

### Scoring Your Audit

| Score | What It Means |
|-------|---------------|
| 90–100% checks passed | Healthy. Schedule next audit in 90 days. |
| 70–89% checks passed | At risk. Fix red-flag items within 2 weeks. |
| Below 70% checks passed | Critical. This room is costing you support hours every week. |

---

### Quick-Fix Reference Card

| Symptom | Most Likely Cause | Fix Time |
|---------|------------------|----------|
| Device offline in TAC | Expired password or network change | 15 min |
| Camera not detected | USB hub power failure | 30 min |
| Can't join meeting from console | MFA blocking resource account | 20 min |
| No meetings showing on console | Calendar sync broken — re-sign in | 10 min |
| Echo on calls | AEC disabled or wrong audio device selected | 20 min |
| App version very old | Auto-update blocked by GPO | 45 min |
