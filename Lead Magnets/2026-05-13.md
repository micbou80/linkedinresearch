# The Teams Rooms Health Audit: 10-Point Checklist to Find (and Fix) Silent Meeting Failures

# The Teams Rooms Health Audit
## 10-Point Checklist to Find (and Fix) Silent Meeting Failures

*Complete this in under 15 minutes per room. Designed for IT Managers at mid-market companies.*

---

### Why this matters
A misconfigured Teams Room doesn't fail loudly. It fails slowly — with people giving up on the technology, reverting to huddles around a laptop, and blaming IT.

This checklist finds the quiet failures before they become loud complaints.

---

### The 10-Point Audit

**[ ] 1. Device is enrolled in the Teams Admin Center**
- Go to: Teams Admin Center → Devices → Teams Rooms
- ✅ Pass: Device appears, status is "Healthy"
- ❌ Fail: Device missing or showing "Offline" / "Unhealthy"
- Fix: Re-enroll the device account; check network firewall rules for Teams endpoints

**[ ] 2. Resource account has a valid Teams Rooms license**
- Check: Microsoft 365 Admin Center → Users → [Room Account] → Licenses
- ✅ Pass: Teams Rooms Basic or Teams Rooms Pro license assigned
- ❌ Fail: No license, or wrong license tier
- Fix: Assign Teams Rooms Pro for managed rooms; Basic for unmanaged/small rooms

**[ ] 3. Camera auto-framing is enabled and tested**
- Test: Stand at the back of the room. Does the camera track and frame you?
- ✅ Pass: Camera adjusts within 3 seconds
- ❌ Fail: Wide static shot, or camera ignores movement
- Fix: Enable Intelligent Camera in device settings; update firmware

**[ ] 4. Audio echo test passes**
- Test: Join a test call (dial the Microsoft test number: +1-323-480-8000). Ask a colleague to listen.
- ✅ Pass: No echo, no clipping, voice is clear at 3 metres from mic
- ❌ Fail: Echo, reverb, or drop-out
- Fix: Check microphone placement (should be < 2.5m from furthest speaker); disable room echo if enabled in acoustic settings

**[ ] 5. One-touch join works for all calendar types**
- Test: Book a meeting from Outlook. Does it appear on the console 15 minutes before?
- ✅ Pass: Meeting appears on screen; join with one tap
- ❌ Fail: Meeting missing, or requires manual entry
- Fix: Check Exchange resource mailbox auto-accept settings; verify calendar sync in Teams Admin Center

**[ ] 6. Content sharing (HDMI/wireless) works without a Teams call**
- Test: Plug in a laptop via HDMI or use wireless share. Does it display without launching Teams?
- ✅ Pass: Content displays within 5 seconds
- ❌ Fail: Blank screen, no signal detected
- Fix: Check HDMI ingest cable; enable "Content only" mode in device settings

**[ ] 7. Room is running the latest certified firmware**
- Check: Teams Admin Center → Devices → [Room] → Device details → Software version
- ✅ Pass: Running latest certified release (check aka.ms/TeamsDevicesRelease)
- ❌ Fail: More than 2 versions behind
- Fix: Schedule auto-update window in Teams Admin Center (off-hours recommended)

**[ ] 8. Front Row or Dual Screen layout is configured for hybrid**
- Check: Is "Front Row" enabled on rooms with dual displays?
- ✅ Pass: Remote participants shown at eye level on the lower screen
- ❌ Fail: Default gallery view only; remote faces in the corner
- Fix: Enable Front Row in Teams Admin Center → Configuration profiles → Meeting settings

**[ ] 9. Room has a defined "room profile" in Admin Center**
- Check: Teams Admin Center → Devices → Configuration profiles
- ✅ Pass: Room assigned to a profile matching its size (small/medium/large)
- ❌ Fail: No profile assigned; running default settings
- Fix: Create 3 profiles (huddle / standard / boardroom) and assign accordingly — takes 20 minutes once.

**[ ] 10. At least one staff member knows how to restart the device**
- Test: Ask the office manager: "If the screen is frozen, what do you do?"
- ✅ Pass: They know: hold power button 10 seconds, or use Teams Admin Center remote restart
- ❌ Fail: "I call IT" (and IT takes 2 hours to respond)
- Fix: Print a laminated 3-step restart card and attach it to the back of the console

---

### Scoring

| Score | Status | Action |
|-------|--------|--------|
| 9–10 ✅ | Healthy | Schedule quarterly re-audit |
| 6–8 ✅ | Needs attention | Fix red items this week |
| < 6 ✅ | At risk | Block 1 afternoon for a full remediation sprint |

---

### Quick-Fix Priority Order
If you only have 60 minutes, fix in this order:
1. License check (5 min) — no license = no features
2. Calendar sync (15 min) — kills adoption fastest if broken
3. Audio echo (20 min) — single biggest meeting quality complaint
4. Firmware update (schedule it, 5 min) — prevents 80% of random failures
5. Restart card (5 min) — zero-cost, immediate impact
