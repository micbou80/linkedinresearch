# The 10-Minute Teams Rooms Health Audit: A Checklist for IT Managers

# The 10-Minute Teams Rooms Health Audit
## A Checklist for IT Managers at Mid-Market Companies

> **Outcome:** In 10 minutes, identify exactly which rooms are costing you money, trust, and meeting time — and know what to fix first.

---

### Why this matters

The average misconfigured or unmonitored Teams Room causes **3–5 failed or degraded meetings per month**.
At an average fully-loaded meeting cost of **$450/hour** and 45-minute average meeting length, that's **$1,012–$1,687 in wasted time per room, per month** — before you count the IT tickets.

Most organisations don't have a hardware problem. They have a **visibility problem.**

---

### Section 1: Device Health (2 min)

- [ ] Every Teams Room device is enrolled in **Microsoft Teams Admin Center (TAC)**
- [ ] All devices show **"Healthy"** status in TAC > Devices > Teams Rooms
- [ ] No device has been offline for >24 hours in the last 30 days
- [ ] Firmware and app versions are within **one release** of current (check: TAC > Devices > Updates)
- [ ] Auto-update is enabled OR you have a scheduled update window defined

**🚩 Red flag:** Any device showing "Non-urgent" or "Critical" in TAC Health with no open ticket.

---

### Section 2: Audio & Camera Configuration (2 min)

- [ ] Default microphone, speaker, and camera are explicitly set in device settings (not left on "Auto")
- [ ] Camera field-of-view covers the full table (verify last saved snapshot in TAC or on-site)
- [ ] No echo cancellation warnings in the last 30-day call quality report
- [ ] Front Row or Gallery view mode matches the room's physical layout
- [ ] Content camera (whiteboard) is configured if whiteboard is present

**🚩 Red flag:** Rooms where "Auto" is selected for audio devices — this causes the most inconsistent call quality complaints.

---

### Section 3: Room Scheduling & Utilisation (2 min)

- [ ] Each room has a **dedicated resource mailbox** in Exchange/M365
- [ ] Room mailbox auto-accepts bookings and has a sensible booking policy (max duration, buffer time)
- [ ] Room is appearing correctly in Outlook/Teams room finder with accurate capacity and equipment tags
- [ ] You have pulled a **utilisation report** in the last 60 days (TAC > Analytics > Rooms & Devices)
- [ ] Rooms with <20% utilisation have been flagged for a "right-sizing" conversation with facilities

**🚩 Red flag:** Any room without a resource mailbox — it can't be booked properly and won't appear in analytics.

---

### Section 4: Call Quality Data (2 min)

- [ ] You have accessed **Call Quality Dashboard (CQD)** in the last 30 days
- [ ] You have filtered CQD by room account UPNs to isolate Teams Rooms call quality
- [ ] Poor call rate for Teams Rooms endpoints is **below 3%**
- [ ] No single room accounts for >30% of your poor call volume (spot the outlier)
- [ ] Jitter, packet loss, and round-trip time are within Microsoft recommended thresholds:
  - Jitter: <30ms
  - Packet loss: <1%
  - Round-trip time: <100ms

**🚩 Red flag:** If you've never filtered CQD by room UPN, you are flying blind on room quality.

---

### Section 5: Security & Compliance (2 min)

- [ ] All room accounts are **cloud-only or hybrid-synced** M365 accounts (no on-prem only)
- [ ] Room accounts have **MFA disabled** only where Microsoft explicitly supports this (resource accounts) — verify your policy is intentional
- [ ] Room accounts are excluded from Conditional Access policies that block device sign-in
- [ ] Device passwords/PINs are managed and rotated per your security policy
- [ ] Teams Rooms devices are in a dedicated **device compliance policy** in Intune/Endpoint Manager

**🚩 Red flag:** Room accounts enrolled in CA policies designed for human users — this causes silent sign-out failures.

---

### Your Score

| Checked items | What it means |
|---|---|
| 23–25 | Strong foundation. Focus on optimisation and utilisation analytics. |
| 17–22 | Moderate risk. 2–3 quick fixes will remove most user complaints. |
| 10–16 | High risk. Prioritise device health and CQD visibility immediately. |
| Below 10 | Critical gaps. Schedule a full audit and consider a managed service. |

---

### Your 3 quick wins (do these first)

1. **Enrol every device in TAC** — free visibility, takes 15 minutes per device.
2. **Filter CQD by room UPNs** — find your worst room in under 5 minutes.
3. **Set explicit audio defaults** — eliminate "Auto" device selection on every room system.
