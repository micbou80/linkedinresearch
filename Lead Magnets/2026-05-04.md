# The 10-Minute Teams Rooms Health Check: A Checklist for IT Managers

# The 10-Minute Teams Rooms Health Check
## A Checklist for IT Managers at Mid-Market Companies

> **Goal:** In 10 minutes, identify the rooms costing you the most in wasted meeting time, failed calls, and shadow IT workarounds.

---

## Part 1: Inventory Reality Check (2 min)

- [ ] Do you have a single source of truth for every MTR device (make, model, firmware version, room name)?
- [ ] Are all rooms listed in the **Teams Admin Center > Devices > Teams Rooms**?
- [ ] Do you know which rooms run **Windows** vs **Android**? (Support paths differ.)
- [ ] Are any rooms still running a firmware version older than 90 days?
- [ ] Do you have at least one room flagged as **"Unhealthy"** in the admin center right now?

**Score: ___ / 5**

---

## Part 2: Meeting Experience Audit (3 min)

- [ ] Can every room join a Teams meeting with **one tap** — no PIN, no manual entry?
- [ ] Is **Direct Guest Join** enabled for Zoom/Webex calls where needed?
- [ ] Is the **front-of-room display** showing the room calendar and upcoming meetings on idle?
- [ ] Are cameras set to **auto-frame / people-tracking** where the hardware supports it?
- [ ] Is the **default microphone** tested and confirmed — no echo, no feedback reported in last 30 days?
- [ ] Are rooms with **more than 8 seats** using an **intelligent speaker** or multi-mic setup?

**Score: ___ / 6**

---

## Part 3: Management & Monitoring (3 min)

- [ ] Is **Microsoft Teams Rooms Pro** (or Pro Management Portal) active on your highest-traffic rooms?
- [ ] Are you receiving **automated alerts** when a room goes offline?
- [ ] Do you have a defined SLA for room remediation? (e.g., critical room back online within 2 hours)
- [ ] Is there a **named owner** per room responsible for user feedback?
- [ ] Are room utilisation reports reviewed at least monthly? (Teams Admin Center > Analytics & Reports)
- [ ] Have you set up **conditional access policies** so room accounts can't be used for email/browsing?

**Score: ___ / 6**

---

## Part 4: User Adoption Signals (2 min)

- [ ] Do users in your org know how to **start an ad-hoc meeting** from the room console?
- [ ] Have you communicated the **one-tap join** workflow to employees in the last 6 months?
- [ ] Is there a visible **QR code or quick-start card** in each room?
- [ ] Do you track **room utilisation vs. booking rate**? (Empty booked rooms = ghost meetings = cost)

**Score: ___ / 4**
