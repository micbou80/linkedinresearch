### Why this exists

Most IT leaders justify meeting room technology on gut feel or vendor slides.

## Number 1 — Your Daily Meeting Waste Cost

**The formula:**
```
Average salary of meeting attendees (hourly) 
× Average number of people in a meeting 
× Minutes wasted on AV setup and troubleshooting 
÷ 60 
× Number of meetings per day across your organisation
= Daily Meeting Waste Cost (€/$)
```

**Fill in your numbers:**

| Variable | Industry average | Your number |
|---|---|---|
| Average hourly salary per person | €45/hr | _____ |
| Avg attendees per meeting | 6 people | _____ |
| Minutes wasted on AV/setup per meeting | 8 minutes | _____ |
| Meetings per day (org-wide) | 20 meetings | _____ |

**Example output (using averages):**
€45 × 6 × 8 ÷ 60 × 20 = **€720/day** in wasted salary

**Annualised (×220 working days): €158,400/year**

> This single number typically ends the budget debate.

## Number 2 — Your Ghost Room Rate

Ghost rooms = meeting rooms booked but never used (no-shows, forgotten bookings).

**Industry benchmark: 30–40% of all booked rooms are ghost rooms.**

**Formula:**
```
Number of meeting rooms 
× Average bookings per room per day 
× Ghost room rate (use 35% as default) 
× Cost per booking (facility + IT overhead per hour × avg meeting length)
= Daily ghost room cost
```

**Fill in your numbers:**

| Variable | Example | Your number |
|---|---|---|
| Number of meeting rooms | 15 | _____ |
| Avg bookings per room per day | 4 | _____ |
| Ghost room rate | 35% | _____ |
| Facility + IT cost per hour | €25/hr | _____ |
| Average meeting length (hrs) | 1 hr | _____ |

**Example output:**
15 × 4 × 0.35 × €25 × 1 = **€525/day** in ghost room cost

**Annualised: €115,500/year**

> Teams Rooms with occupancy sensors + auto-release eliminates most of this.

---

## Number 3 — Your Hybrid Tax Per Employee

The hybrid tax = the productivity and friction cost paid by remote participants in poorly equipped rooms.

**Signs you're paying it:**
 - Remote participants can't see who's speaking
 - No automatic transcription or meeting recap
 - Remote attendees disengage within 12 minutes (Microsoft research baseline)
 - Decisions get re-litigated after meetings because remote attendees missed context

**Simple estimate:**
```
Number of remote participants per avg meeting 
× Meetings per week 
× Estimated re-work / follow-up hours caused by poor hybrid experience per meeting 
× Average hourly rate
= Weekly hybrid tax
```

| Variable | Example | Your number |
|---|---|---|
| Remote participants per meeting | 3 | _____ |
| Meetings per week (org-wide) | 100 | _____ |
| Re-work hours per meeting | 0.5 hrs | _____ |
| Average hourly rate | €45 | _____ |

**Example output:**
3 × 100 × 0.5 × €45 = **€6,750/week** | **€351,000/year**

> Intelligent cameras + Copilot meeting recap directly attacks this number.

## Number 5 — Your Payback Period

**Formula:**
```
Total Teams Rooms Investment (Number 4)
÷ Annual waste eliminated (Numbers 1 + 2 + 3 combined)
= Payback period in years
```

**Conservative assumption: Teams Rooms eliminates 40% of identified waste.**

**Example using all averages above:**
- Annual waste identified: €158,400 + €115,500 + €351,000 = **€624,900**
- Conservative 40% elimination: **€249,960 savings/year**
- Investment (15 rooms, mixed): ~€150,000
- **Payback period: ~7 months**
