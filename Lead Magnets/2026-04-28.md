# The Teams Rooms Health Audit: 10 Checks in 10 Minutes

# The Teams Rooms Health Audit: 10 Checks in 10 Minutes

**For IT Managers, AV Integrators, and MSPs**
Run this before your next leadership meeting — or before you lose another deal to 'the room wasn't working.'

---

## Why This Matters

The average mid-market company has 8–25 meeting rooms.
Each room that fails costs ~15 minutes of executive time per incident.
At a blended rate of €75/hr, that's **€18.75 per failure** — before you count the trust damage.

Most failures are preventable. Most IT teams find out too late.

This audit takes 10 minutes. Run it weekly.

---

## The 10-Point Checklist

### 🔌 Hardware & Connectivity
- [ ] **1. Device online?** Confirm Teams Rooms device shows 'Healthy' in Teams Admin Center (TAC) → Devices → Teams Rooms.
- [ ] **2. Camera functional?** Join a test call and verify video feed is clean — no freeze, no black screen.
- [ ] **3. Audio passing?** Test microphone pickup at 3m distance. Verify no echo or clipping.
- [ ] **4. Display output active?** Confirm front-of-room display is on and receiving signal. Check HDMI/USB-C handshake if dual-screen.

### 🛡️ Software & Updates
- [ ] **5. App version current?** In TAC, check Teams Rooms app version. Flag any device >2 versions behind current release.
- [ ] **6. Windows update status?** Confirm OS is not pending a restart. Pending restarts cause mid-meeting crashes.
- [ ] **7. OEM firmware current?** Check Logitech Sync / Poly Lens / Crestview or equivalent OEM portal. Firmware gaps = known bugs staying alive.

### 📅 Room Booking & Calendar
- [ ] **8. Resource account sign-in valid?** In TAC, confirm no 'Sign-in error' on the room resource account. Expired passwords kill calendar sync silently.
- [ ] **9. Room calendar visible in Outlook?** Book a 5-minute test meeting and verify it appears on the room console within 60 seconds.

### 📊 Reporting & Alerting
- [ ] **10. Alerts configured?** Confirm at least one admin receives email or Teams notification when a device goes offline. If no alert exists — you're flying blind.

---

## Quick Fix Reference

| Issue | First action |
|---|---|
| Device offline in TAC | Restart device via TAC remote action — wait 3 min |
| Resource account sign-in error | Reset password + re-authenticate in TAC |
| App version outdated | Push update via TAC → Devices → Update |
| No alerts set | TAC → Notifications & alerts → Rules → New rule |
| Calendar not syncing | Check Exchange resource mailbox permissions — AutoAccept must be enabled |

## Scoring Your Rooms

**10/10** — Healthy. Document and use as your baseline.
**7–9/10** — At risk. Schedule fixes this week.
**Below 7** — Critical. This room will fail in a meeting that matters.
