# The Teams Rooms Health Audit: A 15-Minute Checklist for IT Managers

# The Teams Rooms Health Audit
## A 15-Minute Checklist for IT Managers

> **Outcome:** In 15 minutes, know exactly which of your meeting rooms are costing you money, killing productivity, and about to fail — before your CEO notices.

---

### Why this matters

The average mid-market company (200–2,000 employees) runs **12–40 meeting rooms**. Each room that fails during a meeting costs an average of **$1,200 in lost productivity** (15 people × 8 minutes of troubleshooting × average loaded salary). That's not a hardware problem. That's a management problem.

This checklist gives you a repeatable audit you can run monthly in under 15 minutes using Microsoft Teams Admin Center + Pro Management Portal.

---

### SECTION 1: Device Health (5 minutes)

Go to: **Teams Admin Center → Devices → Teams Rooms**

- [ ] Filter by **Health Status: Unhealthy or Non-urgent** — how many rooms appear?
- [ ] Check **Last Seen** column — any device offline for >24 hours?
- [ ] Check **Peripheral Health** — camera, microphone, speaker all showing green?
- [ ] Any device running on a **firmware version older than 90 days?**
- [ ] Any device with **0 meetings in the last 7 days?** (Ghost room — likely broken or unused)

**Your number to track:** ___ rooms flagged as unhealthy today: [ __ ]

---

### SECTION 2: Meeting Reliability (5 minutes)

Go to: **Teams Admin Center → Analytics & Reports → Device Usage** (or Pro Management Portal if licensed)

- [ ] Which 3 rooms have the **lowest join success rate** this month?
  1. _______________________
  2. _______________________
  3. _______________________
- [ ] Any room with a join success rate **below 85%?** Flag for immediate investigation.
- [ ] Check **Call Quality Dashboard** — any rooms with consistent audio/video quality issues?
- [ ] Are any rooms consistently **joining meetings late** (>30 seconds after scheduled start)?

**Your number to track:** Average join success rate across all rooms: [ __ ]%

---

### SECTION 3: Configuration & Security (3 minutes)

- [ ] Are all room accounts using **Resource Accounts** (not personal accounts)?
- [ ] Is **Auto-accept meeting invitations** enabled on all room accounts?
- [ ] Are devices enrolled in **Microsoft Intune** for remote management?
- [ ] Is **conditional access policy** applied to room accounts?
- [ ] Are any rooms still on **legacy Skype for Business** mode? (Kill this now.)
- [ ] Do all rooms have a **named owner or responsible team** in your asset register?

---

### SECTION 4: Utilisation Reality Check (2 minutes)

- [ ] Pull room booking data from **Exchange / Outlook** for the last 30 days
- [ ] Any room booked **>80% of working hours?** → Capacity problem
- [ ] Any room booked **<20% of working hours?** → Consolidation candidate
- [ ] Are **no-show rates** tracked? (Rooms booked but meeting never joined via Teams)
- [ ] Do you have **enough huddle spaces** (1–4 person rooms) vs. boardrooms?

### Benchmark: What good looks like

| Metric | Poor | Average | Best-in-class |
|--------|------|---------|---------------|
| Device health (green) | <70% | 80–90% | >95% |
| Join success rate | <80% | 85–92% | >97% |
| Room utilisation | <25% | 40–60% | 65–75% |
| Avg. firmware age | >120 days | 60–90 days | <30 days |

---

### Quick wins you can action today

- **Enable Teams Rooms Pro Management** — automated alerts mean you know about failures before users do
- **Set up a weekly health digest** in Teams Admin Center (email report, 2 minutes to configure)
- **Create a shared Teams channel** for your AV/IT team with automated device alerts piped in via Power Automate
- **Book a 30-min monthly audit** in your calendar using this checklist
