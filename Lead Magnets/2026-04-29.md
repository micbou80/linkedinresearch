- **One-touch join** eliminates cable/connection friction → saves 6–10 min/meeting
- **Intelligent camera + audio** means remote participants join as equals → fewer repeat questions, shorter meetings
- **Room utilisation data** (via Teams Admin Center / Metaroom) identifies ghost bookings → reclaim 15–20% of room capacity
- **Auto-diagnostics** reduce IT support calls for AV issues → saves IT team 2–4 hrs/week
