## STEP 4: The Hidden Multipliers

The calculator above only captures **direct time waste**. Add these if you want the full picture:

- **Productivity drag**: Meetings that start late rarely recover. Add 15% to your waste figure.
- **Remote participant drop-off**: When audio/video fails, remote attendees disengage. Decisions get revisited. Estimate 1 follow-up meeting per 5 failed meetings.
- **IT support tickets**: Average Teams Rooms support ticket = 45 min of IT time. How many do you log per month? × 12 × IT hourly rate.
- **Perception cost**: Every broken meeting erodes trust in IT. Hard to quantify. Impossible to ignore.

**Adjusted total estimate:**
```
Step 2D result × 1.15 + follow-up meeting cost + IT ticket cost
= £/€/$ _______ true annual cost
```

## STEP 5: What Good Looks Like

Organisations running **Microsoft Teams Rooms with proactive monitoring** (via Teams Admin Center or a managed service) typically report:

- **Tech failure rate drops from ~30% to under 5%**
- **Average meeting start delay: from 4–7 min to under 60 seconds**
- **IT ticket volume for rooms: down 60–80%**

Apply those benchmarks to your numbers:
```
Target waste after MTR optimisation:
Step 2D result × 0.05 ÷ 0.30 (or your failure %)
= £/€/$ _______ residual waste

Annual saving:
True annual cost − residual waste
= £/€/$ _______ per year
```
