### Why this exists

Most Teams Rooms proposals die in finance — not because the numbers are bad, but because nobody did the numbers.

This calculator gives you 5 concrete inputs and 1 output: a defensible ROI figure you can put in front of a CFO.

Time to complete: **under 10 minutes.**

## STEP 1 — Calculate your meeting friction cost

**Input A:** Average employee fully-loaded hourly cost (salary + benefits ÷ 1,760 working hours)
> Rule of thumb for mid-market NL/EU: €55–€80/hr | US: $60–$90/hr
> Your number: **€ ______**

**Input B:** Average attendees per meeting
> Your number: **______** (typical range: 4–8)

**Input C:** Minutes lost per meeting to AV setup, bad audio, latecomers waiting
> Industry benchmark: **8.6 minutes** (Frost & Sullivan, 2023)
> Your number: **______ minutes**

**Input D:** Meetings per room per day
> Your number: **______** (typical: 4–6)

**Input E:** Number of meeting rooms
> Your number: **______**

---

### 🔢 Formula: Annual Meeting Friction Cost

```
(A) × (B) × (C ÷ 60) × (D) × (E) × 220 working days
= Annual cost of meeting friction
```

**Example calculation:**
- €65/hr × 5 attendees × (8.6 ÷ 60 hrs) × 5 meetings/day × 10 rooms × 220 days
- = €65 × 5 × 0.143 × 5 × 10 × 220
- = **€51,194/year** — from friction alone

**Your result: € ______/year**

## STEP 3 — Calculate payback period

```
Payback (months) = Total deployment cost ÷ (Annual friction cost ÷ 12)
```

**Example:** €42,000 deployment ÷ (€51,194 ÷ 12) = **9.8 months payback**

**Your payback period: ______ months**

## STEP 4 — Stack the secondary gains (choose what applies)

These are harder to quantify but real. Check what applies to your organisation:

- [ ] **Reduced IT support tickets** — One-touch join eliminates most AV calls. Benchmark: 2–3 hrs IT time saved per room per month.
- [ ] **Hybrid meeting equity** — Remote participants report 34% higher engagement in Teams Rooms vs laptop-only calls (Microsoft WorkLab, 2024). Proxy value: retention and productivity.
- [ ] **Real estate optimisation** — Teams Rooms usage data (via Pro Management Portal) shows actual room utilisation. Most organisations find 20–30% of rooms are underused — data to right-size your real estate.
- [ ] **AI meeting features** — Copilot in Teams Rooms (recap, action items, live translation) saves ~15 min of follow-up per meeting. At 5 meetings/day/room: 75 min/day saved per room.
- [ ] **Compliance and recording** — Automatic transcription reduces manual note-taking and supports audit trails.

## Red flags to address before you present

- [ ] Do you have a room booking system (or plan for one)? Without it, utilisation data is lost.
- [ ] Is your Teams tenant on a plan that includes Teams Rooms licensing, or will you need add-ons?
- [ ] Who owns AV support post-deployment — IT, AV integrator, or MSP?
- [ ] Have you baselined current room utilisation? (Use sensor data or manual audit first.)
