# Research Brief

## Optimization Objective

Maximize LinkedIn post engagement rate:

```
engagement_rate = (likes + comments + shares) / impressions × 100
```

Secondary signal: comments count (highest-quality engagement).

## Target Audience

- IT Managers and IT Directors at mid-market companies (200–2,000 employees)
- AV Integrators and MSPs selling Microsoft Teams Rooms
- Technology decision-makers evaluating Microsoft 365 / collaboration tools
- Microsoft partners and resellers

## Content Themes

1. Microsoft Teams Rooms — implementation, ROI, troubleshooting, best practices
2. Microsoft 365 productivity and adoption
3. Modern workplace and hybrid work technology
4. MSP / integrator business growth and revenue strategies
5. AI in the modern workplace (Copilot, AI-powered meetings)

## Success Thresholds

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Engagement rate | > 3% |
| Comments per post | > 5 |
| Profile visits uplift | Measurable week-over-week |

## Content Constraints

- Every post must offer or reference a lead magnet
- Lead magnets must be genuinely useful, not just gated filler
- No clickbait or manufactured controversy
- One post per day, published manually by the user
- Posts must be authentic — grounded in real practitioner expertise
